## Daily Feeding
- **Time:** Morning (7:00 AM) and Evening (6:00 PM).
- **Locations:** Starting from Main Gate -> Library -> Hostels -> Canteen.
- **Protocol:** Ensure fresh water is available at all spots. Report any leftover food.

## Medical Emergencies
If you spot an injured animal:
1. **Do not panic.** Approach slowly.
2. Call the emergency helpline immediately: **+91-9876543210**.
3. Send a photo and location to the WhatsApp group.
4. Wait for the medical team if safe to do so.

## Reporting
- Use the **Log Book** in the club room to record feeding completion.
- Note any behavioral changes in specific dogs.

## Code of Conduct
- Always wear your ID badge during rounds.
- Be polite to other students/staff who may have concerns.
- Do not engage in arguments; report conflicts to the Core Team.
